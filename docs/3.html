Pangea
: A monstrous landmass, all the continents as one. What a place it must have
been, it’s space deﬁned only by the lines of nature.
A Eutopia interrupted by an entropy that pulled the continents apart shaping our world.
This ﬁrst continent, now fragmented, is but a story that still breathes and lives, surfacing
through the distant veins of impressions that remain hidden within earth’s body, remind
-
ing us of the unity that once was.
Pangea
isn’t only a story of a distant space, it is a testament to the relentless spirit of
friendship.
We invite you to travel into our conceptual space, where we’ve bound our creative forces
across geography into these pages. We wish you a pleasant journey.
